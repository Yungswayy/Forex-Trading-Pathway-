Notes

Quick Definitions

Oversold

(Usually RSI under 30)

Market is stretched down. This is context, not an automatic buy signal.

Overbought

(Usually RSI above 70)

Market is stretched up. This is context, not an automatic short signal.

Divergence

(Price and RSI disagree)

Bullish divergence = price lower low, RSI higher low. Bearish divergence = price higher high, RSI lower high.

Workflow Cards

1D Context

Start here every morning
  • Near major demand → possible reversal zone
  • Near major supply → possible rejection zone
  • Middle of nowhere → no edge yet
  • Deep RSI stretch → context only, not entry

4H Structure

This decides the trade bias
  • HH + HL → bullish structure
  • LH + LL → bearish structure
  • Choppy overlap → low conviction / no-trade
  • At key level → wait for confirmation

1H Refinement

Use only after 4H gives the setup
  • Acceleration into level → be careful of chasing
  • Stalling at level → better for timing
  • Divergence → supportive, not primary
  • Weak bounce → supports continuation short
